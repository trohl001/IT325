I Love to Garden.

FRUIT TREES - We love being able to pick fresh juciy fruit from our trees. Every year we plant a few more fruit trees. I'm planting 2 new apple trees this spring. Dry figs and fig jam are amazing!! Fig trees are easy to care for here in Virginia Beach but all the other fruit trees take a lot of love and watchful eyes. We try to use natural solutions for pest control but it's a lot of work and I need to admit the pest might be winning the battle.
